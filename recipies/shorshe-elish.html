Shorshe Elish

Description

The most beloved way to prepare Ilish fish, where the richness of the fish is balanced by a sharp, creamy mustard seed gravy.

Ingredients

  • Hilsa fish steaks
  • Yellow and black mustard seed paste
  • Turmeric
  • Green chilies
  • Raw mustard oil

steps

  1. Prepare the Mustard Paste: Grind yellow and black mustard seeds with a pinch of salt and two green chilies into a smooth paste. Adding the chilies prevents the mustard from turning bitter.
  2. Marinate the Fish: Lightly rub the Hilsa steaks with salt and turmeric powder.
  3. Temper the Oil: Heat mustard oil in a pan until it smokes slightly. Lower the heat and add nigella seeds (kalo jeera) and split green chilies.
  4. Simmer: Mix the mustard paste with a little water and pour it into the pan. Bring it to a gentle simmer.
  5. Cook the Fish: Carefully slide the fish steaks into the gravy. Cover and cook for about 5â€“7 minutes on each side.
  6. Finish: Drizzle a tablespoon of raw mustard oil over the top right before turning off the heat for that authentic sharp aroma.
