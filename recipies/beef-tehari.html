Beef Tehari

Description

A fragrant and spicy rice dish made with Chinigura rice, tender beef pieces, and a heavy emphasis on mustard oil and green chilies for that signature kick.

Ingredients

  • Chinigura rice
  • Beef cubes
  • Mustard oil
  • Yogurt
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • Blend of warm spices (cardamom, cinnamon, cloves).

steps

  1. Marinate the Beef: Mix small beef cubes with ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, salt, and a spice blend of cardamom, cinnamon, and mace. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the Meat: Heat mustard oil in a heavy-bottomed pot. Saut√© sliced onions until golden, then add the marinated beef. Cook until the meat is tender and the oil separates from the spices.
  3. Prepare the Rice: In a separate pot, lightly fry the soaked Chinigura rice in a bit of ghee and whole green chilies until the grains look translucent.
  4. Combine: Add the cooked beef (along with its flavorful oil) to the rice. Pour in hot water (the ratio is usually 2:1 water to rice).
  5. The Dum: Once the water level drops to the surface of the rice, cover the pot tightly. Lower the heat to the absolute minimum and let it steam (Dum) for 20 minutes until the rice is fluffy and fragrant.
